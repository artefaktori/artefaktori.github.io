Alexa
Aesthetics Sensei

I believe each visual has a story. Through graphic design, I can help you tell your story in the beautiful and eloquent way it deserves to be told. But I also believe design is not only about producing beautiful visuals, it’s about finding solutions to problems a customer may face. Design and problem-solving go hand in hand!

My opinion is that certain design is good if the unity of form and function is achieved and my work is done when every piece has clicked into place.

Jolly
Vector Ninja

I love every part of the creative process and all the unexpected places it can lead you. You can frequently find me sketching, drawing, or working with vector graphics. I love making loud, colorful, and messy designs based on emotions rather than strict formulas. I enjoy mixing and matching different styles to create something new.

It’s surprising how far a single idea can take you once you get the ball rolling.
