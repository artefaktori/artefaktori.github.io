A DUO SHARING A PASSION

We are a freelance couple driven by love of design.

Aleksandar

Digital Designer

I love transforming good ideas into great experiences, creating visual journeys that evoke an emotional response and capture the user's attention. I am extremely curious and inquisitive by nature, and that helps me to thoroughly research a certain area. I am also detail-oriented while keeping my eyes on the big picture at all times. My opinion is that certain design is good if the unity of form and function is achieved.

Andjela

Print Designer

If we compare the design with a delicious meal, the graphic elements represent its spices. I see the design as a creative combination of spices with the goal of creating a unique and unrepeatable taste. Each new challenge is a new shift of boundaries, a new adventure and an opportunity to learn, improve and progress. I am a great lover of nature and I find inspiration in its inexhaustible sources.
